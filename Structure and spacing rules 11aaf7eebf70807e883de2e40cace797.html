Structure and spacing rules

To ensure an optimal experience on mobile devices and larger screens, the spacing rules are defined as follows:

Margins:

  • Between Images and Text:
    • Desktop Version: 20px margin.
    • Mobile Version: 15px margin, to keep the content accessible and easy to read on smaller screens.
  • Between Sections:
    • Desktop Version: 40px margin top and bottom.
    • Mobile Version: 30px margin top and bottom, helping to reduce overflow and maintain clarity.

Paddings:

  • Buttons:
    • Desktop Version: 15px vertical padding and 25px horizontal padding.
    • Mobile Version: 12px vertical padding and 20px horizontal padding, facilitating interaction on touch screens.
  • Content Boxes:
    • Desktop Version: 20px internal padding.
    • Mobile Version: 15px internal padding, to reduce space without compromising readability.

Alignments:

  • Text:
    • Left-aligned in both versions, with titles centered.
  • Images:
    • Center images in content sections with a 20px margin on desktop and 15px on mobile.
  • List Items:
    • Left-aligned with a 15px margin on desktop and 10px on mobile.
