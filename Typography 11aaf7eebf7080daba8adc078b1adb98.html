Fonts

  1. Lora - Regular 400 (Headings, Navigation, Quotes)
    • Why use Lora:
      • Elegance and Formality: Lora is a serif typeface that conveys elegance and authority, which is suitable for titles and headings that should catch the reader's attention.
      • Readability: Its well-balanced design ensures that titles are easy to read, even at larger sizes.
  1. Open Sans (Body Text)
    • Why use Open Sans:
      • Modern and Neutral: This sans-serif font is modern and has a clean design that doesnâ€™t distract from the main content.
      • Readability on Screens: Its clarity makes it ideal for long blocks of text, enhancing the reader's experience.
  1. Montserrat (Buttons and Calls to Action)
    • Why use Montserrat:
      • Attractive Design: Montserrat is a sans-serif font with a geometric style that captures attention, perfect for buttons and interactive elements.
      • Versatility: It offers a variety of weights, allowing for emphasis on different actions.

Sizes

  1. H1 - 40px
    • Why this size:
      • Visual Dominance: The H1 is the main title of the page, so it should be the largest and most eye-catching to immediately grab the reader's attention.
  1. H2 - 33px
    • Why this size:
      • Hierarchy: The H2 is a subtitle that should be smaller than H1 but still large enough to indicate that it is an important section.
  1. H3 - 26px
    • Why this size:
      • Clarity: The H3 is used to break down sections within H2, so it should be smaller yet still clearly visible to guide the reader.
  1. Body Text - 16px
    • Why this size:
      • Reading Comfort: This size is standard and comfortable for most readers, ideal for long blocks of text.
  1. H4 - 22px
    • Why this size:
      • Secondary Subheading: The H4 is smaller than H3 but still large enough to distinguish it from body text and maintain hierarchy.
  1. Quotes - 18px
    • Why this size:
      • Emphasis: A size larger than body text but smaller than headings helps quotes stand out without clashing.
  1. Navigation - 14px
    • Why this size:
      • Functionality: It should be legible but not overshadow headings or the main content.
  1. Buttons / CTA - 18px
    • Why this size:
      • Visibility: A slightly larger size makes buttons more appealing and easier to click, encouraging user interaction.
